# Pizza Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes pizza sales performance using SQL and Power BI. The objective is to identify sales trends, customer ordering behavior, product performance, and revenue-generating opportunities through interactive visualizations and business insights.

## Business Problem

A pizza restaurant wants to understand its sales performance and customer purchasing patterns. By analyzing order data, management can identify best-selling products, peak sales periods, and opportunities to improve profitability.

## Tools & Technologies

* SQL
* Power BI
* Power Query
* DAX
* Microsoft Excel

## Project Workflow

1. Imported raw pizza sales dataset into SQL.
2. Performed business analysis using SQL queries.
3. Calculated KPIs and sales metrics.
4. Connected analyzed data to Power BI.
5. Built interactive dashboards and visual reports.
6. Generated business insights and recommendations.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Revenue
* Total Orders
* Total Pizzas Sold
* Average Order Value
* Average Pizzas Per Order

## Dashboard Analysis

### Revenue Analysis

Tracks overall business revenue and identifies high-performing products.

### Sales Trend Analysis

Analyzes daily and monthly order patterns to identify peak demand periods.

### Category Performance

Compares pizza categories based on sales and revenue contribution.

### Size Performance

Evaluates customer preferences across pizza sizes.

### Product Performance

Highlights best-selling and least-selling pizzas.

## Key Insights

* A small number of pizza varieties contribute a significant portion of total revenue.
* Certain weekdays generate higher order volumes than others.
* Sales activity increases during specific months, indicating seasonal demand patterns.
* Large-size pizzas contribute significantly to overall revenue.
* Top-selling pizzas consistently outperform other products in both quantity sold and revenue generated.
* Some products have low sales volume and may require promotional strategies or menu optimization.

## Business Recommendations

* Increase marketing efforts during peak sales periods.
* Promote high-margin and top-performing pizzas.
* Offer discounts on low-performing products.
* Optimize inventory planning based on demand trends.
* Introduce combo offers using popular pizza categories.
